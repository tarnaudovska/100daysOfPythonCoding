About Me

This is what I do.

After over a decade of experience in the computer graphics (CG) industry, I've discovered a newfound passion for Python programming. Through recent learning experiences, I've come to appreciate Python's versatility in automating tasks and customizing tools, enabling me to creatively solve problems and optimize workflows. This revelation has inspired me to fully transition into Python programming and software engineering, where I am eager to contribute my skills to innovative projects and continue to grow in this dynamic field. The prospect of working in Python-driven environments excites me, as it represents a natural progression of my ability to blend technical proficiency with creativity.

To kickstart my Python journey, I immersed myself in two comprehensive Udemy courses: "100 Days of Code: The Complete Python Pro Bootcamp" and "The Complete Python Bootcamp From Zero to Hero in Python." These courses provided me with a solid foundation across a diverse range of tools and technologies. I honed my skills in Python development environments like PyCharm and Jupyter Notebook, learned automation with Python scripting, and explored game development, data science tools like Pandas, GUI creation with Tkinter, and web development using Flask, REST APIs, and SQLAlchemy. Additionally, I gained valuable experience in front-end development with HTML5, CSS3, and Bootstrap, as well as backend processes including web authentication and database management. Through this journey, I’ve also embraced version control practices with Git and GitHub to manage my projects effectively.

I am deeply motivated to apply my growing Python expertise in meaningful and challenging contexts. My GitHub portfolio, which showcases my projects and development progress, reflects my dedication to continuous learning and improvement. With a strong technical background and a proven ability to adapt and innovate, I am excited to contribute to impactful software engineering initiatives.
